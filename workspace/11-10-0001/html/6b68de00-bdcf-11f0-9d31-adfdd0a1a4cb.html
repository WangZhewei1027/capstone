K‑Means Clustering — Interactive Module

Explore how K-Means partitions data into clusters by manipulating points and centroids. Drag points, change K, and step through the algorithm to see assignments and centroid movement in real time.

Learning Objective

After interacting with this module you will be able to:

  • Understand assignment and update steps in K‑Means
  • See how different initial centroids affect convergence
  • Observe inertia (sum of squared distances) changing across iterations

Interaction Design

- Click the canvas to add points. Drag points (or centroids) to see real-time reassignments.
- Use "Step" to run one assign+update iteration. "Play" animates iterations until convergence.
- Toggle "Init by click" to place centroids manually. Shift‑click a point to remove it.

Controls

Tip: Drag points to change cluster geometry. Press Space to toggle Play/Pause.

Layout Description

The module places explanatory text and controls in a left column and the interactive visualization on the right. Safe area margins are 24px and controls maintain at least 16px spacing. The canvas is responsive and supports mouse & touch.
