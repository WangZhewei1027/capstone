The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones, starting from 0 and 1. Discover the mathematical beauty and see it visualized!

Mathematical Foundation

F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2)
Where F(0) = 0, F(1) = 1

Interesting Facts:

  • The ratio of consecutive Fibonacci numbers approaches the Golden Ratio (φ ≈ 1.618)
  • Fibonacci sequence appears in nature: flower petals, pinecones, hurricanes, galaxies
  • Used in computer algorithms, financial markets, and art compositions
