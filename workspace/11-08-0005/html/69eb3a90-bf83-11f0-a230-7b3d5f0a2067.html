How Divide and Conquer Works:

1. Divide: Break the problem into smaller subproblems of the same type.
2. Conquer: Solve the subproblems recursively. If small enough, solve directly.
3. Combine: Merge the solutions of subproblems to solve the original problem.

This visualization demonstrates merge sort, a classic divide and conquer algorithm with O(n log n) time complexity.
