What is Divide and Conquer?

Divide and Conquer is an algorithmic paradigm that works by recursively breaking down a problem into two or more sub-problems of the same or related type, until these become simple enough to be solved directly.

Divide: Break the problem into smaller subproblems
Conquer: Solve the subproblems recursively
Combine: Combine the solutions to get the overall solution

Common Examples

  • Merge Sort
  • Quick Sort
  • Binary Search
  • Strassen's Matrix Multiplication
  • Closest Pair of Points
